🔬 THE SCOUTING REPORT

Barnes sends out Czechia, a squad known for its robust defensive structure and dangerous counter-attacking flair. The Prodigy's key assets include the formidable presence of West Ham's Tomáš Souček in midfield, a tireless engine who dictates play and offers a significant aerial threat. Patrik Schick, when fit, remains a potent striker capable of moments of individual brilliance, and Vladimír Coufal provides a reliable, attacking outlet from right-back. Barnes's game plan often involves a disciplined 4-2-3-1 or 4-3-3, soaking up pressure and then launching rapid transitions. The tactical question for Barnes: Can his Czechia squad break down a potentially stubborn Mexican defense, or will they rely solely on moments of brilliance from Schick and Souček to unlock the scoring?

Will answers with Mexico, a squad famed for its technical quality, quick passing, and passionate play. The Commissioner's assets are led by the veteran leadership and defensive solidity of Guillermo Ochoa in goal, who always seems to perform heroics on the big stage. Upfront, Hirving "Chucky" Lozano's blistering pace and directness are constant threats, while Edson Álvarez provides a crucial shield in midfield. Mexico typically operates with a dynamic 4-3-3 or 4-2-3-1, prioritizing possession and exploiting wide areas. The tactical question for Will: Can his Mexico squad overcome Czechia's physical presence in midfield and defensive organization to create enough clear-cut chances, or will they struggle to convert possession into goals against a well-drilled opponent?
