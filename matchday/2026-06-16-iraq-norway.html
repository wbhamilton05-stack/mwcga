Folks, buckle up! The leaderboard is waiting!

Can Barnes inspire his Iraq squad to break their drawing streak and secure a vital three points?

Warner's tactical challenge will be ensuring his star players are given the freedom to operate while the team maintains structural integrity. Can Warner's Norway replicate their high-scoring performances and overwhelm Barnes's defense, or will the Prodigy find a way to nullify their threats?
