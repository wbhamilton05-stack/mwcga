Türkiye often plays with a possession-based approach, looking to create intricate passing movements. The one tactical question for Barnes: can his midfield maestro's unlock Warner's disciplined defensive lines, or will Türkiye's forward line struggle to convert possession into clear-cut chances against a potentially resolute American defense?

Warner answers with United States, a squad built on athleticism, tenacity, and a direct, high-energy style. The Young Phenom will be leveraging the incredible engine of players like Weston McKennie in the midfield, driving forward and disrupting Barnes's rhythm. The USA typically operates with a pragmatic approach, focusing on quick transitions and exploiting space. The one tactical question for Warner: can his American stars maintain their defensive shape and prevent Barnes's creative players from dictating terms, while simultaneously unleashing their blistering pace on the counter to capitalize on any lapses from the Turkish backline?
